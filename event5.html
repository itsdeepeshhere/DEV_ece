FAREWELL for ECE batch 2018-2022, Mtech ECE batch 2020-2022 (Conducted On-8th April, 2022)

"A sweet ending to a new beginning"
Someone said the easiest thing in the world is to study, though with the 2 year gap in physical interaction with students here came the FAREWELL for ECE batch 2018-2022, Mtech ECE batch 2020-2022.

As a student, no matter how successful you'll become, you will never be going to find the luxury of studying from the teachers and yes we missed too, standing on the podium, delivering lectures to students with amazing memories from students instead of sitting in front of a laptop.

Thanks to all the faculty members, students for joining the eve. This batch will remain special to us, we broke all the records in terms of placement from this batch and we hope the following batches will maintain the same.
Farewell is an emotion, From the heart of the whole Dept of ECE; We wish you all the very best for future endeavours. Looking forward to staying in touch with all of our students. Our doors will always remain open for all the students.
